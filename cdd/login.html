Welcome to The Hour Club, the Vacheron Contantin Collector Club

OPEN

For 260 years the most prestigious collectors have had their names inscribed in our archives…join them and become part of Vacheron Constantin’s unique history.

Owning a Vacheron Constantin timepiece is an encounter. Not only is it an experience to wear one of the world's most finely crafted timepieces made by a Maison having over 250 years of continuous activity, but it is an experience to belong to a family of unique collectors.

As a Vacheron Constantin owner, you are a member of a community of like-minded and passionate people who enjoy the finer things in life. As such, with The Hour Club, Vacheron Constantin wishes to offer you - our collectors - an even more intense experience and a unique opportunity to have access to information, services, and events that are unique for Vacheron Constantin owners.

We invite you to join us and take your Vacheron Constantin journey to another level. It is our pleasure to welcome you to The Hour Club!
